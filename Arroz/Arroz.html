Arroz Alá Iagô!

This mode was made by Iago Rodrigues.He is a professional chef.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of raw rice
  • Water
  • Salt and garlic
  • Chopped Garlic

How to prepare

  1. Smash garlic with salt
  2. Chop the garlic into small pieces
  3. Put the smashed garlic with salt intro pan with butter
  4. After garlic fry you put the raw rice intro the pan
  5. Don't put the water, you need to wait rice fry
  6. After rice fry, you put water with chopped garlic
  7. Taste the water and add salt if necessary
  8. Wait for 10 minutes and verify if the rice is cooked

Congratulations, you did it!
