Lasanha Brasileira

This recipe of Lasanha it's a classic dish, much loved and very easy to prepare. Follow the step-by=step and discover how simple is to prepare a Lasanha that will be a hit on any occasion.

Ingredientes

  • 500g of lasagna pasta
  • 2 cartons of cream
  • 3 tablespoons of wheat flour
  • 500g of mussarela
  • 2 glass of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 500g ground beef
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 500g of ham
  • 1 grated onion
  • 1 carton of tomato sauce
  • 1 packet of grated cheese

  1. Lasanha

    Cooking the pasta accordgin to the manufacture's introductions, pour it into a baking dish filled with cold water so it doesn't stick and set aside

  2. Bolognese sauce

    Sauté the garlic, onion, ground beef, tomato sauce, cook for 3 minutes and set aside

  3. White sauce

    Melt the margarine, add the 3 tablespoons of flour and stir.

  4. Gradually pour in the milk and continue stirring.

  5. Finally, add the cream, stir for 1 minute and turn off the heat.

  6. Assembly

    Pour some of the bolognese sauce into a baking dish, half the pasta, half the ham, half the mozzarella, all the white sauce and the rest of the pasta

  7. Repeat the layers up to the edge of the container.

  8. Finish with the grated cheese and bake in a preheated high oven (220°C) for about 20 minutes.
